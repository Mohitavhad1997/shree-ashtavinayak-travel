Our story

Twenty-nine years of arranging journeys, one group at a time

Shree Ashtavinayak Travel has been organising tours across India since 1996, run today by Sachin Prakash Shimpi and Sharvil Sachin Shimpi from our office in Kalyan.

।। श्री स्वामी समर्थ ।।

Complete client satisfaction, at an affordable price

Given our experience and expertise, we've been arranging tours across multiple states throughout India for close to three decades. From a single Ashtavinayak departure, our catalogue has grown to cover pilgrimage circuits, hill and heritage tours, and international trips — while staying true to the same principles: affordable pricing, reliable service and solutions built around your group.

We're a registered tour operator and travel agency, and we organise domestic group tours all over India as well as international packages.

Our services

Hotel Booking & Tour Packages

Domestic group tours organised across Himachal, Uttarakhand, South India, Rajasthan, Kerala, the North-East, Kashmir, Madhya Pradesh, Leh-Ladakh and more.

Pilgrimage Tours
