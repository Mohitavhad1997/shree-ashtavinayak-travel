Our namesake journey

The Ashtavinayak circuit, in order

Eight temples, one continuous route across Maharashtra. This is the trip we were founded to run — the sequence below is the same order pilgrims have followed for generations.

01

Moreshwar, Morgaon

The starting and closing point of the yatra.

02

Siddhivinayak, Siddhatek

Set on the bank of the Bhima river.

03

Ballaleshwar, Pali

The only shrine named after a devotee.

04

Varadavinayak, Mahad

A modest temple with a golden dome.

05

Chintamani, Theur

Once visited regularly by the Peshwas.

06

Girijatmaj, Lenyadri

Carved into a hillside cave, reached on foot.

07

Vighnahar, Ozar

Known for its striking gem-set eyes.

08

Mahaganapati, Ranjangaon

The final stop, closing the circuit.
